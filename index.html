Dance calendars

I maintain ICS calendars for a few dance series. You can subscribe to them to get the whole dance series in your calendar app. I hope you find them useful.

Follow the instructions for the calendar app or software you use.

Google Calendar Apple Calendar Microsoft Outlook Outlook on the Web Other

Google Calendar

As of 2018, you still can’t subscribe to calendars directly from the mobile app. Instead, you have to use a computer to add the calendar and it will sync to your phone/tablet through your Google Account.

  1. Open Google Calendar in a new tab.
  2. In the sidebar, click the + beside “Add a friend’s calendar”.
  3. From the menu, click From URL.
  4. Copy the URL for the calendar you want here:
  5. Paste the URL in the form, then click Add Calendar.
  6. That’s it! The calendar might take a few hours to sync to any mobile devices on the same Google Account.

Apple Calendar

  1. Click the link for the calendar you want to subscribe to:
    Ottawa Contra Dance
    ContraMontreal (français)
    ContraMontreal (English)
  2. Your browser will ask if it’s okay to open the link in Calendar. The specific message depends on your browser. Click Allow or OK.
  3. Calendar will open with a panel telling you to enter a calendar URL, but the input field will already be filled out correctly. Click “Subscribe”.
  4. Calendar will take a moment to subscribe, then it will open the info panel for you to confirm the calendar settings. You can just click OK and accept the defaults. But if you want to change the calendar colour or sync it on iCloud, you can set that here.
  5. That’s it! It might take a minute before events show up, but just closing and re-opening Calendar seems to get it going properly.

Microsoft Office Outlook

Confusingly, Microsoft also uses the name “Outlook” for their online web-based services Outlook.com, Outlook on the Web, and Office 365. If you use Outlook in a browser, follow the instructions for Outlook on the Web instead.

This should work for most recent versions of Outlook, at least 2007–2016.

  1. Click the link for the calendar you want to subscribe to:
    Ottawa Contra Dance
    ContraMontreal (français)
    ContraMontreal (English)
  2. Your browser will ask if it’s okay to open the link in Outlook. The specific message depends on your browser. Click Allow or OK.
  3. Outlook will show a prompt asking if you want to “add this Internet calendar to Outlook and subscribe to updates”. Click Yes.
  4. That’s it! Outlook might show the calendar beside your other calendars. You can switch to a single-calendar view with events from all calendars by turning on Overlay mode from the toolbar’s View tab.

Outlook on the Web

Confusingly, Microsoft also uses the name “Outlook” for the desktop mail and calendar program that’s part of Microsoft Office. If you use Outlook not in a browser, follow the instructions for Outlook (Desktop) instead.

  1. Open your Outlook calendar in a new tab.
  2. In the top bar, click “Add calendar”, and from that menu, click “From Internet”. A calendar subscription sidebar will pop out on the right.
  3. Copy the link for the calendar you want here:
  4. Paste the link in the “Link to the calendar” field of the Calendar subscription sidebar. You can fill out the calendar name too if you want. If you leave the calendar name blank, it will automatically use the name of the dance (“Ottawa Contra Dance” or “ContraMontreal”). Then click Save.
