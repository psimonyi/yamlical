Google Calendar

As of 2018, you still can't subscribe to calendars directly from the mobile app. Instead, you have to use a computer to add the calendar and it will sync to your phone/tablet through your Google Account.

  1. Open Google Calendar in a new tab.
  2. In the sidebar, click the + beside "Add a friend's calendar".
  3. From the menu, click From URL.
  4. Copy the URL for the calendar you want here:
  5. Paste the URL in the form, then click Add Calendar.
  6. That's it! The calendar might take a few hours to sync to any mobile devices on the same Google Account.

Microsoft Office Outlook

This is based on documentation for Outlook 2007. But it will probably work in other versions too.

  1. Click the link for the calendar you want to subscribe to:
    Ottawa Contra Dance
    ContraMontreal (français)
    ContraMontreal (English)
  2. Your browser will ask if it's okay to open the link in Outlook; click yes.
  3. Outlook will show a prompt asking if you want to "add this Internet calendar to Outlook and subscribe to updates"; click yes to that too.

Otherwise, click Cancel on opening the link (or "Launch Application" or whatever your browser is showing) and open your calendar app.

Look for a way to subscribe to a calendar by URL or on the Web. Often if you can find a list of your calendars there will be a button nearby to add another. It will ask for a URL or calendar address.
